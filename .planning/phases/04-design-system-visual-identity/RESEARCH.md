# Research: Phase 4 — Design System & Visual Identity

**Goal:** Research the design strategy for "Deep Reasoning" AI products like OmniMind, focusing on transparency and agentic UX.

## 1. Design Philosophy: "The Glass Box"
The 2026 aesthetic for AI agents shifts from "magic" (black box) to **Radical Transparency**.
- **System 1 vs. System 2:** Visual distinction between fast retrieval and deep reasoning.
- **Cognitive Rhythm:** Motion and streaming text reflect the "pulse" of the model's thought process.

## 2. Brand Tokens & Colors
The "Neural Dark" palette reduces eye strain during long-context audits.
- **Primary:** Obsidian/Deep Charcoal (`#0A0A0B`)
- **Reasoning:** Violet/Deep Blue (`#5D3FD3`) — Internal monologue, thinking states.
- **Action:** Amber/Gold (`#FFBF00`) — External actions (API, files) requiring human-in-the-loop (HITL).
- **Surface:** Glassmorphic layers (refractive depth) to show "depth of thought."

## 3. Visualization: Reasoning Tree
Instead of a flat chat, reasoning is visualized as a **collapsible hierarchy**.
- **Branching Paths:** Showing alternative hypotheses the agent considered.
- **Confidence Heatmaps:** Highlighting specific points in the output based on internal probability.
- **Status Indicators:** Pulsing (Active), Stippled border (Hallucination risk), Verified (Check).

## 4. Agent Personalities (Visual Identity)
Each personality (Coder, Analyst, Researcher, ArchTech, Critic) needs a distinct visual marker:
- **Coder:** High-contrast, monospace elements, "Terminal Green" accents.
- **Analyst:** Data-grid inspired, "Structured Blue" accents.
- **Researcher:** Search-bar/Book icons, "Knowledge Indigo" accents.
- **ArchTech:** Structural/Blueprint motifs, "Architectural Gray" accents.
- **Critic:** Shield/Cross-check icons, "Audit Red/Orange" accents.

## 5. Web Frontend: Adaptive Bento
- **Generative Layouts:** The UI "grows" components (data grids, terminals) based on the agent's current task.
- **Spatial Anchoring:** Keeping context pinned while streaming new thoughts.
